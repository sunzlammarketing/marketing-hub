SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is not about "tricking Google" — it is **how you help Google understand and trust your content**. Done right, it gives you a free, stable traffic source that compounds over time, unlike ads that stop the moment you stop paying.

This article lays out a roadmap for your first 30 days, written for someone starting from zero.

## Week 1: Understand users and pick keywords

Everything starts with **search intent**. Before writing anything, ask: what does the person typing this keyword actually want?

- **Informational**: "how to run Facebook ads" → they want to learn.
- **Transactional**: "buy cheap running shoes" → they want to purchase.
- **Navigational**: "Shopee login" → they want a specific site.

> Picking the wrong intent means writing an article nobody needs. This is the most common beginner mistake.

Free tools to start with: **Google Keyword Planner**, Google's autocomplete suggestions, and the "People also ask" box.

## Week 2: On-page optimization

On-page covers everything you control directly on the page:

1. **Title tag** containing your main keyword, under 60 characters.
2. **Meta description** that is compelling and earns the click.
3. **Clear heading structure**: one H1, with logically nested H2/H3.
4. **Short URLs**, hyphen-separated, no special characters.
5. **Image optimization**: meaningful file names and filled-in `alt` attributes.

## Week 3: Quality content

Google increasingly rewards **E-E-A-T**: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness and Trustworthiness. Good content needs to:

- Fully answer the reader's question.
- Include examples, data and real experience.
- Be easy to read: short paragraphs, bullet points, supporting visuals.

## Week 4: Links and measurement

- **Internal links**: connect related articles across your own site.
- **Backlinks**: earn links from reputable sites — focus on quality over quantity.
- **Measurement**: set up **Google Search Console** to track rankings and impressions.

## Conclusion

SEO is a long game. Do not expect to rank overnight — but if you do the right things consistently for 3-6 months, you build an asset that brings in customers for free every day. Start today with **one keyword and one genuinely good article**.
